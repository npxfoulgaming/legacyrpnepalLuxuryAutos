Luxury Autos Vehicle Purchasing Guide

At Luxury Autos, you can acquire vehicles from exclusive categories, including Respected, Heroic, Legendary, Special, Casino, and Rare. All vehicles are available for purchase with real money, whether you are buying a single vehicle or a bundle.

How to Purchase:

  1. Create a Ticket: To begin the purchase process, create a ticket on our Legacy Roleplay Nepal Discord Server.
  2. Submit Vehicle Details: Attach the name and pictures of the vehicle(s) you wish to purchase. Be sure to specify the exact model from our catalog.
  3. Provide Payment Confirmation: Include a screenshot of your payment transaction for verification. Ensure that your payment matches the total amount of the vehicles you selected.
  4. Share Your In-Game Contact: Provide your in-game phone number to help us confirm and process your order efficiently.

Delivery Process:

Your vehicles will be delivered via cargo shipment at Los Santos International Airport. You will receive advance notifications in two ways:

  • Discord Ticket: Updates will be posted in your ticket, so you’ll be informed of the shipment’s status.
  • In-Game Call or Message: You’ll receive a call or message in-game when the cargo ship is about to land.

Once the shipment arrives, you will be invited to the airport to receive your Car Keys, completing your order. Our team ensures a smooth and prompt delivery to guarantee your satisfaction.

Payment Method

We offer several convenient payment options, including eSewa, Khalti, IME Pay, and Direct Bank Transfers. For payments via QR code, you can Click Here to access the QR code.

After completing your payment, please take a screenshot of the transaction confirmation along with the Name and Picture of the Car you wish to purchase. Submit these details by creating a support ticket in the Luxury Autos channel on Discord. Our team will verify your payment and process your purchase as soon as possible.

No Refund Policy

By choosing to support the server through purchases, you acknowledge and agree that no refunds will be provided under any circumstances. This includes, but is not limited to, situations where the server undergoes a wipe or if you are banned for violating server rules. Any purchases made, such as vehicles or other in-game items, are final and non-refundable, regardless of changes to your account status or the server environment. Please ensure you are aware of this policy before making any transactions.
